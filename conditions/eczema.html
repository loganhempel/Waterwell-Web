Eczema

Calming eczema from the inside out.

Eczema isn’t just a skin problem — it’s your body signalling that something inside is out of balance. Creams and steroids quiet the surface, but the flare-ups keep returning until the internal drivers are addressed.

The Waterwell approach

We start by understanding the type(s) of eczema you have, your individual triggers, and the causes behind them. From there we work to rebalance the gut and immune system — the systems most often driving the inflammation — so your skin has the chance to settle, and stay settled. Kohei has treated hundreds of eczema patients and lived through severe eczema himself, so the care is grounded in clinical experience and genuine understanding.
