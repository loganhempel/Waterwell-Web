A clear path from flare-ups to healthy skin.

No jargon, no guesswork — five considered steps that take you from where you are now to skin that looks after itself.

01
Step 01 · Where we begin

Identifying your skin condition

Understanding what you’re dealing with is the single most important step in your healing journey — starting in the wrong direction costs time, effort and money. We combine in-depth consultations, a range of functional tests, and over a decade of clinical experience to pinpoint exactly what’s going on.

02
Step 02

Removing the obstacles

Food and environmental sensitivities are like speed bumps — they cause ups and downs and slow your progress, quietly fuelling inflammation so there’s little room to heal. Clearing them reduces inflammation significantly and paves a smoother path to healthy skin.

03
Step 03

Treating the underlying cause

Once the inflammation has settled, we address the root of your condition — because permanently avoiding allergens isn’t solving anything. We find what caused those sensitivities in the first place and bring it back to balance, with natural remedies that restore gut function, hormones, detox pathways, your microbiome, stress response and skin integrity.

04
Step 04

Reintroduction

True health isn’t avoiding your triggers — it’s being strong enough to tolerate them. Once your symptoms have eased and the cause is addressed, we gently reintroduce these foods and habits. You’ll be surprised how well your body copes when it’s back in balance.

05
Step 05

Maintenance

Your journey isn’t over when treatment ends — some say that’s where it truly begins. You’ll use everything you’ve learned to hold your progress, and we provide a maintenance plan and check in from time to time, so you never slip back to where you started.

My eczema story
“Eczema is not just a skin condition. It’s an internal condition. I know, because I have suffered from it myself.”

Before I became a naturopath, I was covered head to toe in eczema. It grew so severe I had to resign from my job, and was bed-ridden for nearly five months — living on steroids and antibiotics, in constant pain from dry, weeping, itchy skin. It can only be described as agonising, torturous and hopeless.

When the steroids stopped and every medical avenue was exhausted, a naturopath took me through a consultation that changed everything. A year and a half later, through nutritional, dietary and lifestyle support, my skin was 99.9% eczema-free. That experience became my lifelong passion for natural medicine.

Today I’m the founder and director of Waterwell — a holistic skin clinic on a mission to care for people going through the same hardship I did, or to help others avoid it altogether.
